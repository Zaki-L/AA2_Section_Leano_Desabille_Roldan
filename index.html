Verci is accepting new students!

Verci Public Highschool accepts new residents all year round. All you have to do is make your way up Lily Hill. Verci high does not have a selection process besides a few basic requirements to make sure you fit the environment of Verci.

Requirements include:

Be human (exceptions made for pets and very, very well-behaved creatures).
Be alive (physically).
Be uninfected (ex-infecteds are allowed, but must pass the physical exam).
Be silly.
